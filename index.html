FINTECH ANALYSIS

Tools Used: PowerBI, DAX

In this project, I analyzed how FinTech product development initiatives perform from planning to execution. Despite structured budgets, timelines, and risk ratings, many projects still experienced cost overruns, schedule slippage, and delayed milestones. By connecting project-level variance with labor efficiency and milestone risk, the analysis uncovered that delivery gaps were driven by unrealistic planning assumptions, uneven productivity, and regulatory bottlenecks. The dashboard highlights where execution breaks down and reveals early indicators that signal future overruns before projects are completed.

AMAZON SALES REPORT

Tools Used: PowerBI, SQL(MySQL)

In this project, I analyzed 128,975 Amazon India sales records to uncover not just how much revenue was generated, but where operational gaps were affecting performance. While total sales reached ₹79M, deeper analysis revealed fulfillment failures, high-return product categories, courier inefficiencies, and revenue concentration across specific states. By cleaning and standardizing raw data in SQL and translating it into an interactive Power BI dashboard, the project highlights where revenue leaks occur, which segments drive value, and how shipping and customer behavior impact overall profitability.

AGRICULTURAL SUPPLY CHAIN

Tools Used: Microsoft Excel(Power Query & Pivot Table)

I analyzed 5,000 agricultural supply chain records across West Africa (2021–2025) to uncover how logistics and post-harvest losses affect profitability. Using Excel, I cleaned, modeled, and visualized the data in three interactive dashboards: regional performance, crop-level insights, and logistics efficiency. The analysis highlights profit leaks, identifies the most efficient crops and countries, and shows how transport choices and infrastructure conditions influence margins, providing actionable insights for supply chain optimization.
